EventOps Portal

EventOps is an integrated campus event management platform designed to simplify creating, exploring, and participating in academic, technical, and cultural activities. Built with modern web development practices, it features automated liveness and readiness monitoring designed to showcase containerized deployment and CI/CD automation pipelines.

The system allows event organizers to coordinate details, manage capacities, and export secure registration details, while offering participants a fluid overview dashboard, list filters, and instant QR-code registration badges.
